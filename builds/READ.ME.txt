In this directory the most common builds can be found, ready to program as hex-file 
in an ATMEGA328p with factory fuse settings. 

23nbfm_<board versioin>_<pll>_<f reference>.hex
board version is 1 for 1.x, and 2 for 2.x and up.

for example:
23nbfm41_1_4153_13MHz.hex: board version 1.x, PLL ADF4153 with 13MHz reference frequency.
